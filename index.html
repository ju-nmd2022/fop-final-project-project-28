Backstory

There once was an evil wizard called Everlore. He wanted all fairies and their magic all to himself. One by one the fairies turned into blobs with no magical powers left as Everlore absorbed it all himself. Now he has captured you and he turned you into a blob! You must score as high as possible to get a chance to escape Everlore's tower. Jump on the bricks as they get closer, but watch out for the occasional icicles that he sends down. Good luck!
